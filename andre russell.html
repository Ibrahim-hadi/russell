About Andre Russell

Andre Dwayne Russell (born April 29, 1988) is a Jamaican professional cricketer who represents the West Indies in international cricket. Known for his explosive batting and fast bowling, Russell has established himself as one of the most feared T20 players in the world.

Standing 1.83m tall, Russell's powerful physique allows him to hit enormous sixes and bowl at speeds exceeding 140 km/h. His aggressive style has made him a fan favorite in T20 leagues worldwide, including the IPL, BBL, PSL, and CPL.

Russell made his international debut in 2010 and was part of the West Indies teams that won the 2012 and 2016 ICC World Twenty20 tournaments. Despite injuries limiting his international appearances, he remains one of cricket's most valuable players in franchise tournaments.
